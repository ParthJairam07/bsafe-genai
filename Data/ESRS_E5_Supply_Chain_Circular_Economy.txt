Focus: Supplier Due Diligence and Material Origin

Procurement Standard: Circular Economy & Value Chain (Scope 3)

1. High-Risk Material Audit:

This rule applies to all suppliers providing Semiconductors and Cobalt_Batteries.
2. Mandatory SBTi Verification:

Under the Corporate Sustainability Due Diligence Directive (CSDDD), any supplier of Cobalt must have a verified Science Based Targets initiative (SBTi) status.

Anomaly Action: If sbti_verified is FALSE, the row is non-compliant.
3. Recycled Input Threshold:

To meet LuminaTech’s 2030 Net Zero goal, all battery and casing suppliers must demonstrate a recycled_input_rate of at least 0.20 (20%).

Anomaly Action: Any rate below 0.20 must be flagged for "Supplier Remediation."
